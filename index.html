How We Differentiate: Genuine vs Spoofer

Multi-modal AI fusion across device, behavioral, and environmental signals

✅ Genuinely Stranded Worker Profile

  • 📍 GPS location shows movement history → shelter sought during storm
  • 📱 Delivery app was in foreground for prior 2+ hours
  • 🏃 Accelerometer data shows active movement before weather strike
  • 📦 1–3 active/cancelled orders due to weather conditions
  • 🔋 Battery at 30–60% (actively using phone for navigation)
  • 📶 GPS accuracy degraded due to legitimate storm interference
  • ⏱️ Claim filed 5–30 min after event trigger (human reaction time)
  • 🌧️ Worker's platform shows deliveries dropped to zero in last hour

❌ GPS Spoofer / Fraud Actor Profile

  • 📍 GPS coordinates pinned to disrupted zone, but physically at home
  • 📱 Mock Location API enabled (detected via Android permission flags)
  • 🏠 GPS coordinates match registered home address within 50m
  • 📦 0 active delivery orders — not working, just claiming
  • 🔋 Battery at 90–100% (hasn't been delivering all day)
  • 📡 GPS accuracy is 200m+ (spoofing app limitation)
  • ⏱️ Claims filed within 60 sec of trigger (automated bot behavior)
  • 🚨 50+ workers in the same Telegram group submitting simultaneously

🤖 AI Fusion Logic

GigShield's ML model combines three detection layers into a single FraudScore (0–100):

Layer 1: GPS Analysis (40%)
Mock location detection, coordinate clustering, accuracy analysis, movement trajectory reconstruction
Layer 2: Behavioral AI (35%)
Accelerometer patterns, screen-on time, delivery app foreground time, order activity, claim timing velocity
Layer 3: Syndicate Detection (25%)
Mass submission wave analysis, GPS cluster identification, device fingerprint sharing, referral chain tracking

The UX Balance: Fair for Genuine Workers

How GigShield flags bad actors without punishing honest ones who have poor network or GPS in storms

🛡️ Honest Worker Protections

⛈️ Weather GPS Excuse
GPS inaccuracy during a verified storm → score auto-reduced. Storms genuinely degrade location signals.
🏠 Home = Safe Shelter Logic
If extreme weather blocks a zone, a worker going home is rational. System checks weather API — if confirmed extreme, home GPS is excused.
⭐ Veteran Trust Buffer
Workers with 12+ months, clean record, zero prior fraud flags → minor anomalies are tolerated (soft flag, not suspended).
📞 Appeal Within 24 Hours
Any flagged/rejected claim triggers a WhatsApp notification with a one-tap appeal link. Human review within 4 hours guaranteed.

📊 Decision Threshold System

FraudScore 0–19 AUTO-APPROVED
Payout initiated immediately. No human review. UPI transfer within 2 min.
FraudScore 20–49 SOFT FLAG
Claim APPROVED with enhanced monitoring. Worker notified. Additional checks next 30 days.
FraudScore 50–74 HUMAN REVIEW
Claim held for human investigator review. Worker alerted. Decision within 4 hours. Payout held, not denied yet.
FraudScore 75–100 REJECTED
High-confidence fraud. Claim rejected. Policy flagged. Worker can still appeal — appeals investigated by senior team.
